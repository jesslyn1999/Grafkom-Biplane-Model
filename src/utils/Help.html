HELP MENU

Some useful features:

Key down o   Zoom out

Key down i   Zoom in

Key down x   Rotate around x-axis

Key down y   Rotate around y-axis

Key down z   Rotate around z-axis

Key down up   Move camera up

Key down down   Move camera down

Key down left   Move camera to left

Key down right   Move camera to right
