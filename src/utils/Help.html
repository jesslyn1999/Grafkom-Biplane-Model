HELP MENU

Some useful features:

Press h   View help

Press x   Rotate around x-axis

Press y   Rotate around y-axis

Press z   Rotate around z-axis

Press left key   Rotate camera clockwise

Press right key  Rotate camera counter-clockwise

Press i   Zoom-in the model

Press o   Zoom-out the model

Press r   Reset default view

Key down up   Move camera up

Key down down   Move camera down

Key down j   Move camera left

Key down l   Move camera right
